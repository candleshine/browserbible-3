Psalm 43
1 Judge me, God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation;
2 For you are the God of my strength; why have you thrown me away?
Why do I go mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?
3 Oh, send out your light and your truth; let them lead me;
let them bring me to your holy hill
and to your tabernacles.
4 Then I will go to the altar of God,
to God my exceeding joy;
and on the harp will I praise you, God, my God.
5 Why are you bowed down, my soul?
Why are you disquieted within me?
HJpe in God; for I will yet praise him,
who is my help and my God.
