Psalm 122
A song of ascents, of David.
 
1 I was glad when they said to me,
"Let us go to the house of Yahweh."
2 Our feet are standing
inside your gates, Jerusalem.
3 Jerusalem was built
like a city that is compact together.
4 The tribes go up there, the tribes of Yahweh,
as a law for Israel
to give thanks to the name of Yahweh.
5 The leaders sit on thrones there,
for judgment for the house of David.
6 Pray for the peace of Jerusalem!
They will prosper who love you.
7 May there be peace within your walls
and prosperity within your towers.
8 For my brothers' and companions' sakes
I will now say, "May there be peace in you."
9 For the sake of the house of Yahweh our God,
I will pray for your good.
