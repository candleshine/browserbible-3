Psalm 133
A song of ascents, of David.
 
1 Behold, how good and how pleasant it is
for brothers to remain together in unity!
2 It is like the precious oil on the head
that flowed down the beard,
Aaron's beard,
that flowed down his garments.
3 It like the dew of Hermon,
which comes down upon the mountains of Zion.
For there Yahweh decreed the blessing,
life forevermore.
