Psalm 87
A psalm of the sons of Korah; a song.
 
1 The Lord's city is established on the holy mountains.
2 Yahweh loves the gates of Zion
more than all the tents of Jacob.
3 Glorious things are said of you, city of God. Selah
4 I mention Rahab and Babylon to my followers.
See, there are Philistia, and Tyre, along with Ethiopia.
This one was born there.
5 Of Zion it will be said, "Each of these was born in her;
and the Most High himself will establish her."
6 Yahweh notes as he registers the peoples,
"This one was born there." Selah
7 So also the singers and the dancers say,
"All my springs of water are in you."
