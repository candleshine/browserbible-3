1 When the Sabbath day was over, Mary Magdalene and Mary the mother of James, and Salome, bought aromatic spices, that they might come and anoint Jesus body for burial. 2 Very early on the first day of the week, they went to the tomb when the sun came up. 3 They were saying to one another, "Who will roll away the stone for us from the entrance to the tomb?" 4 When they looked up, they saw that someone had rolled away the stone, which was very large. 5 They entered the tomb and saw a young man dressed in a white robe, sitting on the right side, and they were astonished. 6 He said to them, "Do not be afraid. You seek Jesus, the Nazarene, who was crucified. He is risen! He is not here. Look at the place where they had laid him. 7 But go, tell his disciples and Peter that he is going ahead of you to Galilee. There you will see him, just as he told you."
 
8 They went out and ran from the tomb; they were trembling and amazed. They said nothing to anyone because they were so afraid. 9 8The best ancient copies omit Mark 16:9-20 10 She went and told those who were with him, while they were mourning and weeping. 11 They heard that he was alive and that he had been seen by her, but they did not believe.
